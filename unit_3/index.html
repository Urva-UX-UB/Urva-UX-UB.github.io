Hi, my name is Urvashi!

I design experiences that are easy, intuitive and trustworthy.

To keep digital experiences user-centric, I apply the time-proven success of Design Thinking methodology - I empathize, define, ideate, prototype, test, review and iterate; then on the best idea grows organically. It is this iterative design process of user-centered solution-based thinking that I apply to drive business growth. It also earned me awards, for apparel design.

With a background in the fast paced fashion industry, I remain resourceful under pressure and am forever excited to design with a simple objective of getting the end-user to their goal, and so build brand loyalty.
